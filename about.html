About Us

Content & Co exists to fix the middle. We sit between brands and creators — not as a platform, not as a marketplace, but as partners who ensure integrations actually perform.

01

Why I Started Content & Co

After leaving my previous agency, I wanted something real — work built on actual connection, not just transactions. I wanted to be a real entrepreneur, building relationships between brands and creators that mean something, not just closing deals and moving on.

That's what Content & Co is. Real connections, real integrations, real results.

02

Our Model

We don't believe in retainers or upfront fees. We only win when you win. Our commission comes entirely out of the deal value we secure for you.

This means we are 100% aligned with your goals. If a partnership doesn't close or doesn't generate value, we don't charge. It's a completely risk-free approach to influencer marketing.
